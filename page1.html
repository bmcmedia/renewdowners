About Us

If you are searching for high-end living in an apartment-style home and community, consider ReNew Downers Grove. Our apartments near Wheaton, IL offer a luxury living experience, giving you access to modern conveniences that are unavailable in many traditional housing options. Think about how convenient it would be to have access to numerous elements of resort-style living, all within your own community! Comfort meets elegance in a way you are sure to adore in our studio homes and one- or two-bedroom apartments.

With seven distinct floor plan options available, we are confident you’ll find a layout that feels the most like home to you according to your personal preferences. Having options will let you determine things like the number of closets you have, the square footage of your most-used rooms, and the level of openness to your kitchen layout. 

On top of the physical shape and size of your home, you’ll find luxury amenities in and outside of your apartment that will save you time and facilitate relaxation. When you walk inside your new home, you’ll notice energy-efficient stainless steel appliances and your own washer and dryer, all for your convenience. Take notice of the beautiful view out of the sliding glass doors in your living room leading to a private balcony or patio where you can wind down on warm summer nights. 

Outside of your home, discover refreshing community amenities including a quiet WiFi lounge for those late night work projects, a 24-hour fitness center for accomplishing your health goals, and a resort-style pool and cabana seating for relaxing in the sun. 

Thanks to our ideal location off of I-355 in Downers Grove, you’ll have access to premium shopping, dining, and entertainment options. Additionally, your commute to Downtown Chicago, Northern and Southern Suburbs, or Indiana will be convenient and quick. Enjoy nearby local attractions like the Yorktown Center and the Brio Tuscan Grille. 

You’ll be able to have it all when you live here at ReNew Downers Grove! Give us a call to schedule a tour of your new home.
